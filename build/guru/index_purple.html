Who we are

We’re STEM enthusiasts

About us

Having a background in technology and a wealth of experience from industry across the globe, we offer a wide range of inspiration and practical ideas on tinkering, making DIY projects.

We have the background and pathway to create engaging learning opportunities to help kids develop skills & strategies, prepare them for a complex world, and empower them to use their creativity to solve problems, ask questions, and continually learn while having fun.

Our Motivation

We have discovered that the school's methods of education, which emphasise rote memorisation and standardised testing, stifle children's curiosity. We are motivated in re-conceptualising the learning environment with an emphasis on making-to-learn for digital times.
